Battlegrounds Mobile India, is a popular battle royale mobile game developed by Krafton. It's essentially the Indian version of PUBG Mobile, which was banned in India in September 2020 due to concerns over data privacy and security, as it was developed by a Chinese company, Tencent. BGMI was launched in India on July 2, 2021, after several months of anticipation among Indian gamers. Krafton, the South Korean developer behind the game, made significant efforts to localize the game for the Indian audience and ensure compliance with local regulations. The game features similar gameplay mechanics to PUBG Mobile, where players are dropped onto a remote island and compete against each other until only one player or team remains standing.
